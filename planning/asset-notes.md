# Asset Notes

## App Icon

- Source SVG: `assets/app-icon-source.svg`
- Final user-provided icon PNGs are in `icons/` and should be used as-is.

## Splash Screen

- Logo SVG: `assets/splash-logo.svg`
- Fixed splash background: `#005FBA`
- The splash background is intentionally not part of user-editable theme settings.
- The splash logo uses a transparent background and should be centered on the fixed background.
- Display the logo at 75vw with a sensible max width so it does not fill large screens.
- Center the logo both horizontally and vertically.
